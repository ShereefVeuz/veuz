Build Your Career

Find a Job

Do you have the passion to excel, urge to be creative, a never-ending thirst for knowledge and the desire to grow? Then, Veuz is the right place for you because we focus on building a culture of innovation and breakthrough thinking, which results in value creation.

Application

Our user-friendly career section enables you to apply for the desired job submit your resume with a complete list of credentials and provide detailed information about your experience for the position applied.

Shortlisting

A recruiter or technical panel will review your application, and if your credentials align with our requirements, we will reach out to you for further details.

HR Interview

Here is our chance to get to know each other better. We aim to understand your career goals, skills, strengths, passion etc. You can quiz the recruiter anything you would like to know about the position or Veuz as an organization.

Technical Interview

The technical interview is a golden opportunity for you to exhibit your technical skills. The questions would range across disciplines and may include puzzles, problems and other out-of-the-box questions.

Offer Rollout

Finally, if you are successful, an offer is rolled out along with compensation structure and other rules related to employment at Veuz.
