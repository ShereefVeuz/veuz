What Is ManageEngine?

Zoho Corporation develops the ManageEngine series of enterprise IT management software products, which are designed to simplify the management of IT infrastructure. Among its diverse range of tools, ManageEngine offers a Unified Endpoint Management Solution that plays a crucial role in helping businesses manage and monitor all their IT endpoints from a single, integrated platform. Its aim is to deliver integrated solutions that simplify IT management tasks, enabling organizations to efficiently monitor, troubleshoot, and optimize their IT infrastructure.
